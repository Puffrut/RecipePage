Ingredients

  • 3 Large Eggs
  • 1 Cup Milk
  • 1/2 Cup All Purpose Flour
  • 3 TBSP Unsalted Butter
  • 1 TBSP Vanilla Extract
  • 1 TSP Cinnimon

Optional Ingredients When Serving

  • Chopped Strawberries
  • Blueberries
  • Whipped Cream
  • Ice Cream
  • Maple Syrup
  • Confectioners Sugar

Equipment

  • 12" Oven Safe Skillet (Cast Iron for best results)
  • Mixing Bowl
  • Blender or Whisk

Directions

Step 1

Preheat oven to 425Â°F, place your 12" oven safe skillet onto the center rack to be heated as you mix together the batter.

Step 2

In a suitable bowl, mix together the eggs and milk untill there is no seperation. Then add the flour, cinnimon, and vanilla into the bowl and mix together thoroughly to ensure there is no clumps of flour and all you have is a nice smooth batter.

Step 3

By this time the oven should be preheated and the skillet inside nice and hot. Open the oven, slide out the rack holding the skillet and add the butter to the skillet. When the butter melts completely and begins to sizzle quickly pour the batter directly into the center of the skillet. Slide the rack back in and bake untill the Baby is puffed and golden bown with nice crisp edges. (18-20 minutes)

Step 4

Remove skillet from oven and let it rest briefly. Now is the time to add on your optional ingredients to your liking and enjoy!
